Week 01 · Education
Teach. Build trust.
Become the expert.
6–8 educational posts per month

Education posts show your expertise without selling. They answer the questions your ideal clients are already Googling. Every "How to" post and framework you share makes you the person they think of first when they're ready to buy.

Week 03 · Proof
Show results.
Build credibility.
4–5 proof posts per month

Proof posts convert readers into buyers. They include case studies, client wins, testimonials, and before/after results. You don't need hundreds of clients — one specific result, told with detail, is more convincing than a vague claim of expertise.

Week 04 · Offer
Be clear about
how to hire you.
2–3 offer posts per month

Every 8–10 posts, make your offer crystal clear. Not pushy — just direct. "I help [person] with [problem]. DM me [word] if that's you." Authority without an offer is just a hobby. Most creators never mention what they sell. That's why they stay broke.

Common Mistakes
Why most content
calendars fail.

Mistake 1: Planning 30 days in one sitting. Burnout guaranteed. Plan 1 week ahead, create 2 days ahead.
Mistake 2: Only posting education. You'll build followers, never clients. Mix all 4 themes.
Mistake 3: Making it too rigid. Leave 20% of slots empty for real-time observations — those always perform best.

RulePlan weekly. Create 2 days in advance.Simple
RuleMix: 40% Edu · 25% Story · 20% Proof · 15% OfferMix
