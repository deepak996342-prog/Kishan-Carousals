Myth 01
The Myth
"More followers = more clients."
The Truth
1,000 right followers beat 100,000 wrong ones.

A follower who never buys is just a vanity number.
Targeted content attracts buyers, not just browsers.
Stop chasing volume. Start attracting your ideal client.

Myth 02
The Myth
"You need to post every single day."
The Truth
Consistency beats frequency. Quality beats quantity.

Daily posting burns you out and tanks your quality.
3 great posts per week outperform 7 rushed ones.
Your audience remembers great content. They scroll past filler.

Myth 03
The Myth
"Viral content grows your business."
The Truth
Viral brings views. Authority brings clients.

Going viral once won't pay your rent.
Consistent authority content builds the trust that converts.
A post seen by 500 ideal clients is worth more than
