Formula 02
The Problem–Solver Headline
Helping [audience] stop [painful problem] and start [desired outcome]

Lead with the pain. People scroll until something mirrors their own frustration. When they see their problem named exactly, they stop and read everything. This is the most emotionally magnetic formula.

Helping consultants stop trading time for money and start scaling with content.
