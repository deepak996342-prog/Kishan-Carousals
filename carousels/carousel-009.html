Section 01
Your Headline
The first thing clients read — make it count

Most founders write their job title.
That tells people what you are. Not what you do for them.

❌ Weak
"Founder & CEO at XYZ Solutions"
✓ Strong
"I help [audience] achieve [result] without [pain]"

Section 02
Your Banner Image
Premium real estate 95% of people waste

A blue default banner screams: "I'm not serious."
Your banner is a billboard. Use it to communicate:
who you help, what you do, and one call to action.
Your ideal client should "get it" in 3 seconds.

Section 03
Your About Section
Where curiosity becomes conviction

Don't start with "I am a passionate..." — nobody cares.
Start with the problem your ideal client is facing.
Then explain your solution, your proof, and a clear CTA.
Think: landing page, not resume.

Your Featured Section
Your 24/7 portfolio that pre-sells for you

Pin your 3 best-performing posts or resources here.
A lead magnet. A case study. A carousel that shows your expertise.
Warm leads visit your profile before they DM you.
Give them a reason to stay — and a reason to reach out.

Section 05
Recommendations
Social proof that closes deals before calls even happen

A profile with zero recommendations says: "Trust me."
A profile with 5 client testimonials says: "Here's proof."
Ask your last 3 happy clients for a specific recommendation.
One great recommendation converts better than 10 generic ones.
