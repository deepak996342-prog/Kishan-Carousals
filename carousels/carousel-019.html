Post Type 01
The Personal Story
Highest comments. Highest saves. Every time.

"I failed at X. Here's what it cost me and what I learned."
People share what they feel — not what they know.
A story with a lesson attached outperforms a tips list
by 3–5× on average.
Vulnerability + insight = the most powerful content format.

Post Type 02
The Contrarian Take
Sparks debate. Attracts the right audience.

"Unpopular opinion: [thing everyone believes] is wrong."
This triggers comments from two camps: those who agree
and those who want to argue. Both are good.
More comments = more reach. More reach = more clients.
Just make sure you can back it up.

The Carousel
3× more reach than any other format right now

LinkedIn measures how long someone views your content.
Each swipe = more dwell time = more algorithmic reach.
An 8-slide carousel can earn 5–10× the impressions
of a text post with identical content.
Format matters as much as content.

The Results Post
Converts lurkers into leads faster than anything else

"Client went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]."
Specificity makes it credible. Results make it shareable.
People tag others who need what you provide.
Your clients' wins are your best sales material.
Document them. Post them. Watch leads arrive.

The Question Post
Lowest effort. Highest comment rate.

"What's the one thing you wish someone told you

being asked for their opinion.
Every comment extends your reach. Use questions weekly.
