Format 01 · Carousel
The highest-reach format
on LinkedIn right now.
LinkedIn rewards dwell time. Carousels create it.

Each swipe = more time spent = more algorithmic push. An 8-slide carousel earns 3–5× more impressions than a text post with the same content.
Carousels are also saved at high rates — they become reference material. Saves are the most powerful engagement signal LinkedIn has.

Format 03 · Video
The trust accelerator.
Highest barrier to entry.
Video converts viewers to clients faster than anything else.

Seeing someone speak builds trust at a speed no text can match. A 2-minute talking-head video where you share a real insight builds more credibility than 20 written posts.
But video requires consistency, camera comfort, and production quality. The barrier is high — which means less competition. If you can do it, do it. If not, start with carousels.

Match Format to Goal
Different formats serve
different outcomes.
The right format depends on what you want to achieve.

Want reach and new followers? Carousel or viral text post.
Want authority and trust? Carousel or video.
Want comments and engagement? Text post or question post.
Want to convert warm leads? Video — they need to see you.
Stop asking "what's best." Start asking "what's best for this goal?"

The Winning Mix
Don't pick one.
Combine all three.
A balanced format strategy compounds faster than any single format.

The creators growing fastest in 2025 use all three formats — strategically. The optimal weekly mix: 2 text posts (fast, high comments) + 1 carousel (deep reach, authority) + 1 video (trust, conversion).
This covers reach, authority, engagement, and conversion in one week. It serves different parts of your audience's preference. It maximizes algorithm diversity.
